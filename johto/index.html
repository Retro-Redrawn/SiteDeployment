Johto Redrawn is a collaborative art project by a group of passionate pixel artists from across the world.

The goal was to visually represent the Johto region in a way the original developers could never have achieved with the technical restrictions of the original hardware.

Pan and zoom the map the same you would on sites like Google Maps.

Map requires WebGL compatible browsers - Chrome may require you enable hardware acceleration.
